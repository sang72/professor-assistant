# Week 10 · Session 1: Financial Analysis & Korean Franchise Crisis

## FRM401 | 2026-Fall | Sangho Han

**Topic:** Why Korean franchises struggle: 2024-2026 crisis analysis

---

## Key Crisis Indicators

**Convenience Stores (CU, GS25, Lotte):**
- Franchisee profit margins compressed from 10-12% to 5-7%
- Reason: Real estate inflation + oversaturation
- Franchisee complaints: "Impossible to be profitable"
- Corporate response: Operational efficiency initiatives

**Coffee Franchises:**
- Continued decline from Week 4 Cafe Bene collapse
- Remaining chains struggling
- Reason: Market consolidation, lower customer spending
- Outcome: Further closures expected

**Chicken Franchises:**
- Moderate profitability (10-15M/month)
- Stable but not growing
- Reason: Market maturity, labor cost inflation

---

## Why Korean Franchises Struggling

1. **Real Estate Inflation:** Rents have doubled in key areas (2015-2025)
2. **Labor Cost Inflation:** Minimum wage increased 60% (2015-2025)
3. **Market Saturation:** Too many franchises in same categories
4. **Consumer Preference Shift:** Less eating out, more delivery/at-home
5. **Regulatory Pressure:** New laws protecting franchisees (raising compliance costs)

---

## Financial Stress Symptoms

- Franchisee profitability down 40-50%
- Franchisee lawsuit filings up 300%
- System abandonment up (franchisees closing stores)
- New franchisee recruitment down
- Franchisor cost-cutting (reduced support)

---

## Franchisor Responses

**Good Responses:**
- Help franchisees reduce costs (operational efficiency)
- Renegotiate royalty rates (share burden)
- Invest in brand marketing (increase customer traffic)

**Bad Responses:**
- Ignore problem
- Blame franchisees
- Raise royalties further (makes worse)
