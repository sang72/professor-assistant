## Best Practices

**1. Clear Contractual Expectations**
- Profit projections realistic (not promises)
- Support levels defined
- Royalty justification explained
- Conflict resolution process clear

**2. Regular Communication**
- Monthly franchisor-franchisee updates
- Quarterly financial reviews
- Annual strategic discussions
- Open-door policy for problems

**3. Shared Success Metrics**
- Both franchisor and franchisee profitability targets
- System-wide KPIs with transparency
- Individual store performance tracking
- Benchmarking against peers

**4. Dispute Resolution Process**
- Step 1: Direct negotiation
- Step 2: Mediation (neutral third party)
- Step 3: Arbitration (if needed)
- Step 4: Litigation (last resort)

**5. Franchisee Representation**
- Franchisee association / council
- Voice in system decisions
- Input on major changes
- Elected representative on board (if large franchise)

---

## Korean Industry Evolution

Korean franchising has shifted from:
- "Franchisor makes all decisions" → "Franchisee participation expected"
- "Handshake deals" → "Transparent contracts"
- "Blame franchisee when fails" → "Shared responsibility"

This evolution is healthy and sustainable.
