**Core Principle to Remember**

The principle that ties everything together:

**"Both franchisor and franchisee must profit for a franchise system to be sustainable."**

If you encounter a franchise case (on exam or in real life) and one side is exploiting the other, predict failure.

If both sides are winning together, predict success.

This simple principle explains most of franchising.
