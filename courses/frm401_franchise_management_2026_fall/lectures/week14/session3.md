# Week 14 · Session 3: International Expansion Simulation & Analysis

## FRM401 | 2026-Fall | Sangho Han

**Topic:** Plan international expansion for Korean franchises

---

## Simulation Task

**Scenario:** A Korean chicken franchise wants to expand to Indonesia.

**Task:** Design expansion strategy addressing:
1. Entry mode (direct, master franchising, JV, or company-owned first?)
2. Product adaptation (how to modify for Indonesian palates?)
3. Supply chain (where to source ingredients?)
4. Franchisee selection (what criteria for Indonesian partners?)
5. Financial projections (expected profitability timeline?)
6. Risk management (what could go wrong?)

**Format:**
- Individual analysis or small group (4-5 people)
- 30-minute work, 10-minute presentation
- Focus on realistic assessment (not overly optimistic)

---

## Key Decision Points

**Entry Mode:**
- If franchisor risk-averse: Master franchising
- If wanting control: Company-owned first (3-5 year proof)
- If capital-rich: Direct franchising with careful partner selection

**Product Adaptation:**
- Indonesian prefer less spice than Korea
- Larger portions expected
- Local side dishes important
- Price sensitivity high

**Financial Reality:**
- First 3-4 years: Break-even or small losses expected
- Year 5+: Profitable if system works
- ROI: 20-30% if successful

**Risk Management:**
- Partner/franchisee selection is #1 risk
- Supply chain disruption possible
- Currency volatility managed through pricing
- Political/regulatory risk (choose stable locations)

---

## Post-Simulation Debrief

- Share expansion strategies
- Compare approaches
- Discuss risks and mitigation
- Award best strategy
