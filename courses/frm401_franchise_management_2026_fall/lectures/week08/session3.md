### After the Exam

### Final Note

Remember the core principle of franchising: **Both parties must profit.**

Any franchise system where only the franchisor profits will eventually fail. Sustainable franchising requires mutual success.

This principle ties together everything we've learned. When you encounter a franchise case (on exam or in real life), ask: "Are both sides winning?"

If yes, it's sustainable. If no, it will fail.
